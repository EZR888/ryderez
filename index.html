About Me

I was born in a log cabin in the Kentucky backwoods... Oh, wait, that was Abe Lincoln. Never mind.

I'm a native New Englander, born and raised in Rhode Island, moved to Connecticut, and a few years later, moved to California, eventually landing in Napa Valley. There, I discovered not only great wines, but an interest in near-infrared photography and all it's magic. This is the stuff of the new James Webb Space Telescope, which can see billions of light years away.

I've been out of the galleries for a few years, since moving to the coast, but I will be retiring soon and will have more time to dedicate myself to my photography. I hope you enjoy the sampling of my work I have on this site. I will be adding other, non-infrared, collections as time permits.
