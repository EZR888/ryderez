About

My love for creating and strategizing marketing content began at an early age. When I was fifteen, I started my own personal fitness page on Instagram, with the intention of helping others kickstart their own health and wellness journey. My personal hobbies still include health & fitness, yoga, and outdoor activities.

Using creative software and the skills I learned through attending the University of South Carolina's School of Journalism, I plan to pursue a successful career in creative strategy. I am currently on track to achieve my MS in Data Analytics and Communication at UofSC.

Originally from New England, I am a graduate of the University of South Carolina, with a major in Marketing and Communications. I am a Content Strategist, currently based out of Charlotte, NC.

I look forward to hearing from you!
