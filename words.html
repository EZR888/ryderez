How to Use


Click "Start" to begin word selection.

Use "Refine" to randomly highlight words.

"Reset" will clear your words

You can "Add" words, or full names, one at a time, or stringing them together with commas. e.g. Fred, Barny, Wilma Flintstone, George Washington

You can "Delete" the same way.

You need to do these additions for each computer or phone or tablet you use, but it's a one-time thing and an easy copy/paste
