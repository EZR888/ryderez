Instructions

One Set = 38 Spins
Each spin randomly picks a number from 1 to 38

Run Simulator ignores settings for Multi-Spin
Multi-Spin uses the Number of Runs setting
Run Simulator always uses 38 spins for 1 set
Spin Delay can be set to less than 1. Ex: .01

Tasks

Directional Differences: Measures shifts in distribution of hits within runs.

Top - Bottom: Measures shifts in the balance of hits between the top and bottom halves of the wheel.
Right - Left: Tests for lateral bias (complements top-bottom).
Top Ends - Center: Measures shifts from the center of the top row towards or away from the 5 spaces at the left and the right.
Bottom Ends - Center: Measures shifts from the center of the bottom row towards or away from the 5 spaces at the left and the right.

Variance & Distribution: (Variance, Density, Clustering) Measures key characteristics from sets of data.

Variance: Within-run variance, showing distribution of 38 spins.
Density: Highest frequency for five spaces on the wheel for each run.

Specific Targets: (00 cluster and 0 cluster).

Sum of 00: Consisting of five spaces on the wheel. Identified as the "00 cluster", (double zero and the two numbers on each side: 13, 1, 00, 27, 10).
Sum of 0: Consisting of five spaces on the wheel. Identified as the "0 cluster", (double zero and the two numbers on each side: 9, 28, 0, 2, 14).
